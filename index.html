About Me


PERSONAL: Amanda Lois Henry was born on January 31 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. I spent much of my childhood in northern Virginia before my family moved to Lakewood, Colorado, just before my junior year in high school. I attended the University of Colorado at Boulder, where I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in English Literature and had a Sunday morning music radio show on KUCB. I married my soulmate, Michael Morris, in 2015. We live in Englewood, Colorado with our sixteen-year-old Siamese/Persian mix cat named Milos.

HOBBIES/INTERESTS: I've been a volunteer with Project Angel Heart in Denver for over 23 years. I love their mission of providing folks who have health challenges with home-delivered, healthy, medically-tailored meals to gain strength. I'm learning to play the ukulele and love hiking in the mountains at every opportunity.

PROFESSIONAL: My fantasy occupation would be to provide cartoon voice-overs or have my own show on KEXP or KCRW. I would like to become a full stack web developer.
